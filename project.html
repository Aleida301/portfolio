TRAVELLING THROUGH MUSIC

ABOUT TRAVELLING THROUGH MUSIC

This Product was created with the intention of bringing a wonderful experience to those long roadtrips or even short ones. We provide you with artists from the cities you pass by as you go to your destination. This way you can discover the wonders that surround you. As you pass each city you will be able to travel through music

HOW TO USE

  1. Choose a origin and destination from the map below. (make sure to choose options from the dropdown menu)
  2. Copy all the track codes and click on the spotify link provided.
  3. Log into your spotify account if you are not already logged in
  4. Once you are in Spotify make an empty playlist and just paste the codes.
  5. The tracks should appear and now you are ready to go.
