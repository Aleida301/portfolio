TRAVELLING THROUGH MUSIC

ABOUT(product)

Whit this product we showcase our skills in HTML, CSS and Javacript. This Product was created with the intention of bringing a ownderful experience to those long roadtrips or even short ones. We provide you with artists from the cities you pass by as you go to your destination. This way you can discover the wonders that surround you. As you pass each city you will be able to travel throught the music around you.

How to use

  1. Choose a route from the google maps provided
  2. Click on make playlist

Now everytime you pass a new city songs from local artists will be added to your playlist. You will be able to travel to music. Try it down here.
